# Scope: Version 1

## In Scope
- Internal use only
- Client selection
- Date-range selection
- Channel-level performance summary
- Key metrics such as spend, impressions, clicks, CTR, conversions, CPA, and ROAS where available
- Simple trend comparison with a previous period
- A short insights section
- Source and freshness visibility

## Not In Scope
- Real-time dashboards
- Auto budget changes
- Predictive recommendations
- Full client portal
- Natural language chat
- Complex attribution modeling

## Why
The goal of version 1 is to reduce manual reporting effort and give the team one reliable way to answer the same question. Everything else can come later once the core workflow is validated.
